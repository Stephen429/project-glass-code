Project GLASS: Ushering in a New Era of Transparency at LDVTS

SSLG PMC • 2026-06-28

The Supreme Secondary Learner Government (SSLG) of Lydia D. Villangca Trade School is proud to announce that Project GLASS has officially entered its pre-release phase. This initiative is a flagship project led by the SSLG Secretary, directly fulfilling a core campaign promise to ensure that the student government remains transparent and provides verifiable "receipts" for its actions, ensuring all records are visible and accessible to the student body.

A Vision for Transparency

Project GLASS is designed as a comprehensive transparency dashboard, serving as an open-initiative by the student government. This project arrives as a direct response to nationwide calls from Filipinos demanding greater transparency in government institutions. We believe that if national reform is the goal, we must start at the very least within our own walls, ensuring our student government sets the standard for integrity.

The technical foundation for this dashboard is a direct application of the skills learned by our student leaders during the Leadership Enhancement and Development (LEAD) 2026 seminar organized by Student Leaders for Good Governance. By leveraging the frameworks for ethical leadership, advocacy, and digital navigation taught during this program, we are transforming our commitment to governance into a tangible, digital reality.

What to Expect

As we enter this pre-release phase, our team is focusing on refining the user experience and ensuring the stability of the digital platform. The primary goal is to create a reliable and intuitive space where data is not just presented, but easily understood.

Key features under development include:

  • Centralized Record Access: Providing a clear view of school policies and governance documents.
  • Initiative Tracking: Real-time updates on the status of various student government projects to keep the community engaged.
  • Integrated Digital Assets: Leveraging the design tools used in our official school publications to ensure the interface is both professional and accessible.

Looking Ahead

The transition of Project GLASS from concept to reality is a collaborative effort, reflecting the dedication of the 2026-2027 SSLG officers. As we prepare for the official launch, our focus remains steadfast on the principles of transparency and service that define this administration.

Project GLASS is more than just a website; it is the foundation of a more accountable and connected student community at Lydia D. Villangca Trade School.

LDVTS-SSLG'S Project GLASS
